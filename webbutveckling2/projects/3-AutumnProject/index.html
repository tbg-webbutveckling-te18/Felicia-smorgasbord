The Basics of JavaScript

On this site you will get a basic explanation of some things you can do in JavaScript. If you have any questions feel free to contact me. Contact info can be found under the about me subpage.

First Step

Start by linking in your Js document before the closing tag of the body element in your HTML document. When you have done that go to your Js document and write for example 'console.log("test");' or 'document.writeln("test");'. After you have done that go to your preview and see if the text has shown up either in the inspector (accesible by right-clicking on your page and clicking inspect) or on the page itself. If the text shows up you know that you have connected your Js document correctly.

Now thet we have linked our js document to our HTML document you can go to intro to continue learning JavaScript.
